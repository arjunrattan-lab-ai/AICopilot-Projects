How to Fix a Parser Gap

A parser gap is a mapping problem — the signal exists, Motive just isn't reading it yet.

1
Get the PGN spec
Obtain the J1939 PGN spec for the make from the OEM or by reverse-engineering the CAN bus.
2
Add the mapping
Add the PGN → signal name mapping to Motive's parser for that make/model.
3
Deploy to devices
Push the parser update to all devices running on that vehicle make.
4
Signal flows
Coverage numbers improve in VEHICLE_TELEMATICS_METRICS. AIOC+ starts working on that make.

Telematics / Connected Devices — Pat Miller, Gautam / Hareesh (edge platform)
Timeline
Historically 14–55 days per make. Platform v2 (proactive coverage engine) is designed to bring this down.
