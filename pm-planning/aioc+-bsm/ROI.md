## Revenue Model

BSM is not priced as a standalone add-on. It is the **core AI capability** that turns AIOC+ from a commodity DVR into an AI camera platform.

- **With BSM:** AIOC+ wins in construction, waste, transit segments → ~$3-5M ARR Year 1 (conservative, validated SAM)
- **Without BSM:** AIOC+ competes as non-AI DVR. Win rate drops. Samsara and Netradyne keep the BSM-sensitive pipeline.
- **Win-back potential:** Deals lost to external camera gap (confirmed in win/loss analysis) become winnable once BSM ships.
- **Provision upgrade path:** 268 beta vehicles + future Provision AI customers are natural AIOC+ BSM upgrade candidates.
