## Win/Loss Analysis (Glean)

### Source 1: Preliminary Closed-Lost Opportunity Analysis (Caroline Barragan, Marketing)
- **URL:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1bweydV_uP0GlBZyY0vIOMKgmyCylmPwBe01Cnfk23L4
- **Date:** Sep 2025 (updated Dec 2025)
- **Key finding:** "Lack of External Camera Support" is explicitly listed as a product gap causing ENT deal losses.
  - Quote: "Competitors' ability to offer integrated side and rear cameras with advanced features like backup assist is a key differentiator we lack."
  - Named deal: SFDC 006Vr000003JsarIAC
  - Recommendation 3.1.2: "Dedicate a product squad to achieving feature parity on high-impact competitive differentiators, such as external camera support."
- **Broader context:** Product gaps are the #2 driver of closed-lost deals (behind incumbent advantage). BSM-related gaps (external cameras + backup assist) appear alongside API gaps and integration gaps.
- **Limitation:** The analysis does not break out "blind spot monitoring" or "pedestrian detection" as named loss reasons. External camera + backup assist are the proxies. This is likely a data hygiene issue — the report itself notes "Doing Nothing is a Symptom, Not a Cause" and that many loss reasons are miscoded.
